Graduate Admission

ICST University welcomes applications from qualified candidates seeking to pursue graduate studies. Advance your career with our comprehensive graduate programs designed for professionals and scholars.

Admission Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent from a recognized institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale or equivalent
  • Submission of official transcripts and degree certificates
  • Two letters of recommendation from academic or professional referees
  • Statement of Purpose outlining academic interests and career goals
  • Proof of English proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL) if applicable
  • Passing the university’s entrance test or interview where required

Application Process

  1. Complete the graduate application form online via the university admissions portal.
  2. Upload all relevant academic documents and personal statements.
  3. Pay the application fee of LKR 3,000.
  4. Attend the entrance examination or interview if requested.
  5. Receive admission decision through email notification.

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: September 1, 2025
  • Application Deadline: November 30, 2025
  • Entrance Exams/Interviews: December 10 - 15, 2025
  • Admission Results Announced: January 10, 2026
  • Orientation Week: January 20 - 25, 2026
