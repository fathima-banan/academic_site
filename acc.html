Accreditation & Quality Assurance

ICST University is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence through recognized accreditation and quality assurance frameworks.

National Accreditation

ICST University is fully accredited by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Sri Lanka, ensuring all degree programs meet national standards for quality education and academic rigor.

International Recognition

ELU holds membership and recognition from international higher education bodies, including the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU) and the International Association of Universities (IAU), affirming its global commitment to academic standards.

Quality Assurance Processes

The university continuously implements quality assurance mechanisms, including periodic program reviews, faculty development, and student feedback to ensure continuous improvement and compliance with best practices.
