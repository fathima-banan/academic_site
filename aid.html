Financial Aid

ICST University is committed to making education accessible to all qualified students by offering a range of financial aid options.

Types of Financial Aid

  • Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance.
  • Need-Based Grants: For students with financial challenges; awards are based on income and need.
  • Work-Study Programs: Part-time employment opportunities on campus to help students earn while studying.
  • External Scholarships: Information and assistance provided for applying to scholarships from government and private organizations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Enrollment as a full-time student at ICST University.
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 for merit scholarships.
  • Submission of completed financial aid application and supporting documents.
  • Demonstrated financial need for need-based aid.
  • Compliance with university code of conduct and academic progress standards.

Application Process

  1. Complete the Financial Aid Application form available on the university portal.
  2. Submit proof of income, tax returns, or any other requested financial documents.
  3. Provide academic transcripts and letters of recommendation if applicable.
  4. Attend an interview if requested by the Financial Aid Office.
  5. Await notification of award status via email.

Important Dates

  • Financial Aid Application Opens: September 1, 2025
  • Application Deadline: November 15, 2025
  • Award Notifications: December 20, 2025
  • Appeal Submission Deadline: January 10, 2026
