Health & Wellness

Supporting Your Well-being at ICST University

Comprehensive Wellness Programs

Our university prioritizes the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of students. We offer wellness programs including fitness classes, mindfulness workshops, and nutrition counseling to help you thrive academically and personally.

Students can participate in yoga sessions, meditation groups, and stress management seminars, fostering a balanced and healthy campus lifestyle.

Accessible Health Services

ICST University provides on-campus health services staffed by licensed medical professionals. Our clinic offers routine checkups, vaccinations, and immediate care.

Confidential counseling services are available to support mental health and emotional challenges, ensuring a safe and supportive environment.

Student Support & Resources

Beyond programs and clinics, our dedicated support staff connect students with resources like peer support groups, crisis hotlines, and wellness education campaigns.

We continuously engage the campus community in health awareness events to build resilience and promote lifelong wellness habits.
