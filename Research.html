Research at ICST University

ICST University is committed to advancing knowledge through innovative research and interdisciplinary collaboration. Our faculty and students actively contribute to a broad spectrum of fields, from engineering and technology to social sciences and humanities.

Research Centers & Institutes

  • Center for Sustainable Energy Solutions
  • Institute of Advanced Biotechnology
  • Social Innovation and Policy Research Lab
  • Environmental Science Research Center
  • Digital Transformation and AI Research Hub

Current Research Focus Areas

  • Renewable energy systems and smart grids
  • Biotechnology for health and agriculture
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning applications
  • Climate change mitigation and environmental protection
  • Social policy and community development
  • Cybersecurity and data privacy
  • Robotics and automation
  • Digital economy and innovation management

Opportunities for Students

We encourage students to engage in research projects, internships, and collaborations with industry partners. Graduate and undergraduate students can participate in seminars, workshops, and funding programs to support their innovative ideas.
