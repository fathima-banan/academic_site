Our History

Tracing the legacy of ICST University

Established in 1995, ICST University started as a modest institution committed to providing quality higher education to the eastern region of Sri Lanka. From its humble beginnings, ELU has evolved into a leading university known for its academic excellence, innovative research, and vibrant campus life.

Over the past two decades, the university has expanded its faculties and introduced cutting-edge programs to meet the evolving demands of the global knowledge economy. ELUâ€™s commitment to community engagement and social responsibility has fostered strong partnerships with local and international organizations.

Significant milestones include:

  • 1995: Foundation stone laid and first batch of students admitted.
  • 2000: Establishment of the Faculty of Engineering and Faculty of Business.
  • 2010: Launch of the Graduate Research Center and partnerships with global universities.
  • 2018: Expansion of campus facilities including state-of-the-art laboratories and student centers.
  • 2023: Recognition as one of the top universities in the region for innovation and inclusivity.

Today, ICST University continues to uphold its founding values while embracing future challenges with vision and resilience, ensuring students graduate prepared to lead and innovate in their fields.
