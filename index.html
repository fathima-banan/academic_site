About Our University

We are one of the most dynamic and inclusive universities in Sri Lanka with over 25,000 students locally, and a growing community of international learners from over 50 countries at ICST University.

ICST University was founded to provide quality education for public benefit and global development. With a strong academic heritage and modern vision, ELU offers students a gateway to real-world skills, innovation, and leadership. As a rising institution in higher education, ELU continues to drive impact across the region.

Preparatory Studies

The Preparatory Studies and Skill Development Unit at ICST University is dedicated to equipping students with the essential skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen degree programs and navigate the demands of the professional world.

These programs are especially beneficial for students requiring additional support in academic areas or those transitioning into a new educational system.

Courses include English proficiency, soft skills, technical training, and foundation subjects tailored to each discipline.

With flexible durations—from a few months to a full academic year—these studies ensure a strong foundation for success.

Support services such as academic advising, tutoring, and learning resources are provided to help every student thrive.
