Clubs & Organizations

At ICST University, students are encouraged to join a vibrant community of clubs and organizations that enrich campus life, develop leadership skills, and foster lifelong friendships.

Tech Innovators Club

For students passionate about technology, coding, and innovation. We host hackathons, workshops, and coding challenges.

Drama & Theatre Society

A creative outlet for actors and performers. We organize plays, improv nights, and drama festivals.

Environmental Action Group

Focused on sustainability and green initiatives, we conduct clean-up drives, tree planting, and awareness campaigns.
