Student Housing

ICST University offers comfortable and secure housing options designed to support student success and foster community living.

On-Campus Housing

Our on-campus residence halls provide fully furnished rooms with easy access to academic buildings, recreational facilities, and dining halls. Amenities include:

  • High-speed Wi-Fi
  • 24/7 security and surveillance
  • Common study rooms and lounges
  • Laundry facilities
  • On-site maintenance and support

Off-Campus Housing Support

For students who prefer off-campus living, the university provides assistance to find safe and affordable apartments and homestays near campus. Our housing office offers:

  • Listing of verified rental properties
  • Guidance on lease agreements and tenant rights
  • Connections with local homestay families
  • Transportation options and shuttle services

Application Process

  1. Submit the Housing Application form via the university portal.
  2. Pay the housing deposit to secure your spot.
  3. Receive confirmation and move-in details via email.
  4. Attend the orientation session for residents.
